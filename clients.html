Clients

pricing and specifications

To make transactions and the application of advertisements as smooth as possible we have minimum requirements.

  • Minimun of 100 cars
  • Minimum of a 2 month contract
  • 2×4 ad posters

Company has to come up with ad design and is responsible for production and installation fees Rates for ad spaces calculated based on the minimum of 100 cars. (500 per car).

Option A

  1. Client finds their own graphic designer.
  2. Client gets their own printing company.
  3. Clent ships out the stickers to our company.
  4. We get our subcontracted sticker installation company to apply the sticker to the cars.

Option B

  1. Client get their own graphic designer
  2. Graphic design is then electronically sent to our subcontracted printing company.
  3. Our subcontractor will print and install the stickers.

There will be a retainer fee in both cases. Option A would cost less in terms of the retainer the advertising company would have to pay, because we won’t be responsible for the printing of the stickers. The initial retainer should be received 30 days before the launch of the campaign. The retainer will include the entire amount our company will spend on installation and/ or printing. The retainer amount will be $10,000 for every 100 cars for printing and installation

Specifics of Stickers: All our stickers are going to be no bigger than 2 feet by 4 feet. They are going to be stuck on both sides of the car. They will be covering both front and back doors of the car. These specification will be a fit for any uber x vehicle. The adhesive the stickers will have is called 3M. This adhesive makes the installation and removal of the sticker very easy. It also can be removed at any time without causing any damage to the vehicle or its paint.
